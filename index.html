Ryan P. Creedon (He/Him/His)

A warm welcome to all! I am a young professional pursuing a PhD in applied mathematics at the University of Washington. I investigate nonlinear wave behavior in physical models using a variety of mathematical techniques, from numerical methods to formal asymptotic calculations to analysis. Click here for a copy of my CV, or explore my page below to learn more about my work.

Here's What I Research

Stability of Nonlinear Waves

  • Bifurcations of equilibrium solutions
  • Resonant wave interactions
  • Spectral instabilities of waves
  • Asymptotics of instability growth rates
  • Nonlinear stability of integrable equations

Computing Spectra of Operators

  • Properties of discrete and essential spectrum of operators
  • Asymptotic behavior of spectral elements
  • Numerical computation of spectral elements
  • Behavior of eigenfunctions
  • Nonlinear stability of integrable equations
